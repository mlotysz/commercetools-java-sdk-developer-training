Use Case: Lifestyle and Home Corp

Providing sustainable and eco-friendly home decor, furniture, and other home products.

Product Search

Effective product discovery is essential for a smooth shopping experience. Lifestyle and Home Corp's storefront search is optimized to meet customer expectations:

  • Filter products by attributes like price, size, finish, and eco-friendly features.
  • Segmented into categories such as furniture, home decor, and outdoor.
  • Search results are tailored for different stores.

Dynamic Shipping Options

Lifestyle and Home Corp works with multiple carriers and ensures customers only see valid shipping options:

  • Automatic display all available shipping methods.
  • Filter available shipping methods based on location.

Cart Functionality

Lifestyle and Home Corp implements features to reduce cart abandonment and enhance conversions:

  • Anonymous carts created when a customer adds an item.
  • Customer should have the option to signin/signup and anonymous cart must be merged with the existing customer cart, if there is one.
  • Custom line items for heavy or bulky items with additional charges or handling details.

Order Processing

Orders are processed efficiently with integrated APIs:

  • Integration with fulfillment centers and payment service providers for seamless order processing.
  • Support team ability to make order changes (e.g., update shipping or quantities).

Promotions and Discounts

To engage customers and drive traffic, Lifestyle and Home Corp offers:

  • Welcome coupon codes to boost conversions.
  • Exclusive social media deals to increase interaction and loyalty.

Data Customization Options

The customers should have the option to add custom data to their orders and personal profile:

  • Custom delivery options are instructions can be added to the orders.
  • Customers should have the option to subscribe to promotional newsletters.

Integrations

Lifestyle and Home Corp integrates external services to enhance their eCommerce solution:

  • Connectors for integrations of custom login or third-party services.
  • Automatic customer number generation for new signups.
  • API integration with external services such as fulfillment centers, PSPs, and email services.

Conclusion

By implementing a solution incorporating product search, cart management, and streamlined order processing, Lifestyle and Home Corp will enhance its customer experience and position itself for global expansion in the eco-friendly home products industry.
